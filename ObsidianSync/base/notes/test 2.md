# Решение

$$
\begin{cases}
\left[ \begin{array}{l}
\tan x = a \\
\sin x = a
\end{array} \right. \\
x \neq \tfrac{\pi}{2} + \pi k,\; k \in \mathbb{Z} \\
x \in \left( -\tfrac{\pi}{2}; \tfrac{3\pi}{4} \right)
\end{cases}
$$
