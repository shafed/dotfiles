# Типовик 1

## 1
$$
\begin{gather}
\lim_{ n \to \infty } \frac{2n-1}{3n+2} = \frac{2}{3}  \\
\forall \varepsilon > 0 \; \exists N (\varepsilon) \in \mathbb{R}: \forall n > N(\varepsilon)  \\
\left\lvert  \frac{2n-1}{3n+2} - \frac{2}{3}  \right\rvert < \varepsilon \\
\left\lvert   \frac{6n-3 - 6n - 4}{9n + 6}  \right\rvert < \varepsilon \\
\frac{-7}{9n+6} > -\varepsilon  \\
7 < 9n\varepsilon + 6\varepsilon \\
n > \frac{7-6\varepsilon}{9\varepsilon}
\end{gather}
$$

## 2

$$
\begin{gather}
\varepsilon = 0,01 \\
n > \frac{1}{9}\left( \frac{7}{0,01} - 6 \right)
n > \frac{694}{9} = 77,\dots \\
\text{Ответ: }N = 78
\end{gather}
$$

# Типовик 2

$$
\begin{gather}
\lim_{ n \to \infty } \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{3n} = 0 \\
\forall \varepsilon>0 \; \exists N(\varepsilon) \in \mathbb{R}: \forall n > N(\varepsilon) \\
\left\lvert  \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{3n} - 0  \right\rvert  < \varepsilon \\
\left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{3n} < \varepsilon \\
\frac{1}{2^{3n}} < \varepsilon  \\
\frac{1}{\varepsilon} < 2^{3n} \\
2^{\log_{2}1/\varepsilon} < 2^{3n} \\
\log_{2}1/\varepsilon < 3n \\
n > \frac{\log_{2}{\frac{1}{\varepsilon}}}{3}
\end{gather}
$$

$\varepsilon = 10^{-3}$
$n > 3,3219\dots \implies N = 4$
